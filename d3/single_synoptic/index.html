d3 Prototype 1: Single map; two controls

Prototype viewer that shows only one synoptic heatmap. The left controls which witness is the "source" of comparison, and the right controls which witness is the "target" of comparison. The size of each collation unit is based on number of characters in the target, and the color based on the relative change from the source - more characters removed vs more added.
