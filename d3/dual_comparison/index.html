d3 Prototype 2: Two maps; two controls

Prototype viewer that compares two witnesses. The left controls which witness is the "source" of comparison, and the right controls which witness is the "target" of comparison. The size of each collation unit is based on number of characters in each selected witness, and the color based on the relative change from one witness to get to the other - more characters added vs more removed.
